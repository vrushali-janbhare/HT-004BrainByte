About SmartQueue

SmartQueue is an innovative online doctor appointment and queue management system designed to reduce hospital waiting time. Our platform allows patients to book appointments online, receive queue numbers instantly, and track their live waiting time from anywhere.

We aim to create a smoother, stress-free hospital experience by improving efficiency for both patients and healthcare providers.
